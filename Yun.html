[The Internet]

What is the Internet?

The internet is a network connected globally around the world, of cables physically. Things such as TV, phones, and computers are connected to the internet all the time, and by browsing on a device, we are online.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The internet provides an endless amount of information and answers for people around the world. Things such as Wikipedia and Khan Academy helps inform and educate people.



Why do we need the Internet?

  • Entertainment

    • We in our everyday lives entertain ourselves through the Internet a lot. We use our phones to browse social media and plays video games. We also watch on devices such as TV, which also requires the internet if not downloaded.

  • Emergency Aid

    • Emergencies around the world can be life-threatening. Without the internet, calls on special services such as the firefighters, police officers, and medical personnel cannot be achieved without connection.

  • information

    • The internet also provides us with the storage and sharing of information. It helps us to collect and store historical information, such as online newspapers and special events. This way, the information is more secure and more organized, protected.

  • Connection

    • The internet connects the world, allowing countries to understand and coordinate better together. Even on a small scale, connections happen through sending an email, texting a friend, and many more.
